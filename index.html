What is Cry of Fear about?

The game Cry of Fear takes place in the perspective of protaganist Simon Henriksson, which takes place in Stockholm, Sweden. In the introduction of the game, Simon departs a train commute as he sees a man calling for help around a corner. Upon further investigation, Simon gets hit by a car and falls unconscious. After this incident, the rest of the game follows Simon trying to escape the dark and unfamiliar place he has been brought to. Various monsters/entities continuously attack Simon along his journey.

An important and iconic monologue from Simon during the introduction cutscene sets the game's tone:

“I've always felt alone. My whole life. For as long as I can remember. I don't know if I like it or if I'm used to it, but I know this; being lonely does things to you. Feeling **** and bitter and angry all the time just... eats away at you...”
